Our project consists of creating an events and leisure website, with a broad and dedicated focus on accessibility. We believe this initiative can revolutionize the way we plan, promote and participate in events, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to participate, with a special emphasis on making events accessible. We've implemented affordable ticket booking and purchasing functionality, an inclusive community to promote everyone's participation, and a feedback and continuous improvement system.
